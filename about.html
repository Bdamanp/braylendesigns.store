My Story

Started building. Never stopped.

I got into web design because I love creating things. I'd see local businesses — restaurants, barber shops, contractors — with websites that looked like they were built in 2005. Broken layouts, impossible to use on a phone, no way to contact them.

I thought: I can do better than that. So I started learning.

I taught myself HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern design tools. I studied what makes websites actually convert visitors into customers — not just look pretty, but actually drive results. Every site I build is fast, mobile-first, and designed with one goal: helping your business grow.

Now I'm building Braylen Digital into something bigger. My goal is to be the go-to web designer for small businesses in the Indianapolis area. I offer agency-quality work at prices that actually make sense for local businesses, because I believe every business deserves a great website, not just the ones with massive budgets.

I use cutting-edge AI tools alongside my design skills to deliver sites faster than traditional developers. That speed advantage means you get a professional site in days, not weeks — at a fraction of the cost.
