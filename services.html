Frequently asked questions.

How long does a website take to build?

+

Most basic sites (3–5 pages) are done in 5–7 business days. Larger projects with custom features can take 2–3 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline during our discovery call.

Do I need to provide content and images?

+

It helps if you have your own photos and text, but it's not required. We can write copy for you and source professional stock photography to fill in the gaps.

Will my website work on phones?

+

Absolutely. Every site we build is mobile-responsive from day one. We test on multiple devices and screen sizes before launching.

Do you handle hosting and domains?

+

Yes. We can set up hosting and help you purchase a domain, or work with your existing hosting provider. Hosting is included with most maintenance plans.

What if I want changes after launch?

+

Every project includes revision rounds (1–3 depending on your plan). After launch, sign up for our monthly maintenance plan for ongoing updates, or request one-off changes at a flat hourly rate.

How do payments work?

+

We require 50% upfront to begin work, with the remaining 50% due on delivery. We accept Stripe, PayPal, or bank transfer.

Do you work with businesses outside Indianapolis?

+

Primarily we focus on the Plainfield and Indianapolis area, but we're open to working with businesses anywhere. Everything is done remotely — discovery calls, design reviews, and delivery are all handled online.
